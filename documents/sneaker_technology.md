# How to Start and Maintain a Sneaker Collection

## Getting Started

### Define Your Collection Goals
**Purpose**:
- Personal enjoyment
- Investment potential
- Fashion and style
- Cultural appreciation

**Focus Areas**:
- Specific brands (Nike, Adidas, Puma)
- Specific models (Air Jordan, Yeezy, Air Max)
- Specific eras (vintage, modern)
- Specific styles (basketball, running, lifestyle)

### Set a Budget
**Financial Planning**:
- Monthly purchasing budget
- Emergency fund for rare finds
- Research resale prices
- Know your retail prices

**Saving Strategies**:
- Save for hype releases
- Buy during sales for general releases
- Prioritize must-haves
- Sell unwanted shoes to fund new ones

## Building Your Collection

### Where to Buy

**Retail Sources**:
- Brand websites (Nike.com, Adidas.com)
- Official stores (House of Hoops, Adidas flagship)
- Department stores (Foot Locker, JD Sports)
- Boutique shops (Sneaker stores)

**Secondary Market**:
- StockX (Authenticated)
- GOAT (Authenticated)
- eBay (with authentication)
- Facebook Marketplace (local)
- Sneaker Con (in-person events)

### Grading and Condition

**Condition Definitions**:
- **Deadstock (DS)**: Never worn, perfect condition
- **VNDS (Very Near Deadstock)**: Worn once, like new
- **Near Deadstock (NDS)**: Light wear, clean
- **Gently Worn**: Minimal wear, good condition
- **Used**: Regular wear, signs of use
- **Beaters**: Well-worn, daily use

### Authentication

**Check Authenticity**:
- **Materials**: Correct quality and feel
- **Stitching**: Even, consistent patterns
- **Color**: Accurate shades and hues
- **Logo**: Correct size and placement
- **Box**: Original box and accessories
- **Label**: Correct style codes

**Red Flags**:
- Price too good to be true
- Strange material smell
- Poor construction quality
- Inconsistent sizes
- Missing original accessories

## Maintaining Your Collection

### Storage

**Climate Control**:
- **Temperature**: 60-75°F (15-24°C)
- **Humidity**: 40-50%
- Avoid direct sunlight
- Away from heating/cooling vents

**Storage Solutions**:
- **Original Boxes**: Best protection
- **Clear Display Boxes**: Showcase collection
- **Shoe Trees**: Maintain shape
- **Acid-Free Paper**: Prevent yellowing

**Avoid These**:
- Plastic bags (trap moisture)
- Direct sunlight (causes yellowing)
- Extreme temperatures
- Damp locations (mold risk)

### Cleaning

**General Care**:
- **Regular Cleaning**: After each wear
- **Immediate Attention**: Spot clean stains
- **Gentle Approach**: Don't scrub aggressively
- **Air Dry**: Never use heat

**Specific Materials**:
- **Leather**: Wipe with damp cloth, condition
- **Suede**: Use special brush, protect with spray
- **Mesh**: Use soft brush, soap solution
- **Knits**: Machine wash (cold, delicate)
- **Soles**: Magic eraser, cleaning solutions

**Cleaning Kits**:
- Reshoevn8r (Premium)
- Jason Markk (Trusted)
- Crep (Protection)
- Angelus (Customization)
